# Digital Forensics Investigation

Detailed post-mortem analysis of a multi-stage compromise.

## Process:
- **Evidence Acquisition**: Created bit-for-bit hardware images of affected drives.
- **Timeline Reconstruction**: Analyzed registry hives and event logs to trace lateral movement.
- **Reporting**: Generated a technical intelligence report for executive review.
